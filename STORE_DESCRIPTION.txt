Medical Surgical Procedure Guide is an educational tool designed for healthcare professionals and medical students. 

FEATURES:
- Step-by-step procedural workflows.
- Real-time peer-review interface via LiveKit integration.
- Secure authentication via Google Sign-In.

IMPORTANT LEGAL DISCLAIMER:
This application is for educational and UI/UX automation demonstration purposes ONLY. It does not provide clinical diagnoses, medical advice, or treatment plans. All clinical decisions must be made by a licensed healthcare professional. Use of this app does not establish a doctor-patient relationship.
